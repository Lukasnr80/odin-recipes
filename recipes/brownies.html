Gooey Brownies Recipe

First we want to brown our butter in a pan while we add our sugar to a bowl. Then combine the suagr and butter while adding in our eggs. Mix until you have a fluffy brown consistency. Add in your flour, chocolate chips, vanilla and salt.

After we've mixed our ingredients, we want to preheat the oven to 325ºF, add our batter to a buttered 8x8 pan, and throw them in the oven. Take them out after 40 minutes and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 200g butter
  • 150g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 200g flour
  • 1g salt
  • 2tbsp vanilla
  • 100g chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325ºF
  2. mix dry ingredients (flour,sugar,salt)
  3. brown butter
  4. mix in eggs,butter,vanilla
  5. add chocolate chips
  6. add batter to 8x8 glass pan
  7. cook in oven for 40 min
